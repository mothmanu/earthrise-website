Our Team

Five students, one mission.

EarthRise brings together storytellers, organizers, community-builders and presenters — each carrying their own reason to fight for a cleaner Rwanda.

Patrick Rwema

Videographer & Editor

Patrick is EarthRise's visual storyteller, bringing our journey to life through videography and editing. With a keen eye sharpened by his passion for graphic design, he ensures every frame of our story is as compelling as the mission behind it.

Awan Liakat

Operations & Coordination

Awan is the organizational backbone of EarthRise, drawing on past work experience to keep the team structured, focused, and on schedule. His interest in psychology gives him a unique understanding of what drives people — and what drives change.

Erica Atete

Community & Outreach

Erica is the heart of EarthRise's people-first approach, using her strong interpersonal skills to build genuine connections with communities and stakeholders. Her passion for supporting underprivileged children reflects the deeper purpose behind everything she does.

Liam Onyango

Videographer & Creative Artist

Liam brings an artist's perspective to EarthRise, capturing our work through both a camera lens and a creative eye. A poet and swimmer outside the studio, he approaches every project with rhythm, depth, and originality.

Obichukwu Nwosu

Presentations & Technical Lead

Obichukwu is EarthRise's voice in the room, translating the team's ideas into clear, persuasive presentations. With a passion for hardware programming and a competitive streak from the basketball court, he pairs technical depth with the drive to deliver.
