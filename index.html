A Student Think Tank · Kigali, Rwanda

Turning Kigali's organic waste into income for the people who sort it.

Our mission is to improve waste management in Rwanda by promoting waste-to-energy practices that reduce waste, especially in rural communities — starting with the 70% of Nduba Landfill's intake that never needed to end up there.

What we do

From a single overflowing bin to a circular economy

Most households in Kigali have one trash can, so nothing gets sorted — and compostable waste that farmers would pay for rots in a landfill instead. EarthRise closes that loop.

🗑️

The Problem

Organic waste isn't sorted at the source. Households and businesses miss out on compost income while Nduba Landfill overflows and Rwanda's waste system pays the cost.

Read our root cause analysis →
♻️

Our Solution

Color-coded 100% compostable bags for households — Green for organic, Blue for inorganic — plus heavy-duty multi-bin systems for businesses, all connected to farmers who buy the compost.
